About Little Lemon

A family tradition of authentic Mediterranean cuisine

Based in Chicago, Illinois, Little Lemon is a family-owned Mediterranean restaurant, focused on traditional recipes served with a modern twist. The chefs draw inspiration from Italian, Greek, and Turkish culture and have a menu of 12-15 items that they rotate seasonally.

The restaurant has a rustic and relaxed atmosphere with moderate prices, making it a popular place for a meal any time of the day.

Our Story

Little Lemon opened in 1995 by two Italian brothers, Adrian and Mario, who moved to the United States to share their family's recipes from their hometown in Italy.

Over the years, they have built up a loyal following of food lovers who return again and again to enjoy the homemade pasta, bread, and traditional dishes prepared with love and care.

Today, Little Lemon continues to serve the same authentic recipes while embracing modern culinary techniques and local ingredients.
